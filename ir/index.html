Connecting Industry and Academics

Industrial Relations is a cornerstone committee which works toward building bridges between the the technical committees of IEEE and the industry. It is in charge of organizing and planning the corporate outreach and professional development programs. It shares responsibilities for general and technical fundraising events.

This committee helps build the necessary connections between the companies and the students at Purdue. Interested in promoting job opportunities, holding a company info session, or more? Email us today!
