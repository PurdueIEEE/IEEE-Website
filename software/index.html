FAQs:

Q: I am not in the College of Engineering, can I still join?
A: Any Purdue student may join, regardless of major, college, or class standing.
Q: How much time does this program require?
A: Aside from the two hour weekly learning session, most people spend only 1-2 hours reviewing material.
Q: How much prior experience do I need?
A: Absolutely none. We will teach you what you have to know. If you are familiar with web development, we do offer an intermediate track that may be more your style.
Q: What is this certificate you mentioned?
A: Any attendee who completes our final project will recieve a digital certificate from the College of Engineering, with a QR code to verify your achievement.
